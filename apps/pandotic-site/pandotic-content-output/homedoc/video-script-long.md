# HomeDoc — Long Video Script (90 seconds)

*Tone: Founder-friendly, conversational, grounded. Suitable for website hero video, pitch deck walkthrough, or LinkedIn.*

---

**[OPENING HOOK — 10 seconds]**

Every homeowner has a drawer full of documents they'll never look at again — warranties, inspection reports, utility bills, insurance policies. And somewhere in all that paper is information that could save them thousands of dollars or keep their family safe.

**[THE PROBLEM — 15 seconds]**

The truth is, most people don't know their home. They don't know if their water heater is past its lifespan. They don't know their wildfire risk score. They've never compared what a heat pump would actually cost them over 25 years versus patching their old furnace. The information exists — it's just scattered everywhere.

**[WHAT WE BUILT — 25 seconds]**

HomeDoc changes that. It's a platform that uses AI to read your home documents — utility bills, inspection reports, appliance labels, warranties — and turns them into structured, actionable data. It scores your environmental risks by pulling from five federal data sources. It tracks every appliance and system in your home. It calculates the true cost of upgrades with real incentive data. And it plans your maintenance automatically.

**[THE "AHA" MOMENT — 15 seconds]**

Here's what's different: we don't use one AI model for everything. We use four, each matched to its strength. A fast, cheap model reads your utility bill. A reasoning model analyzes your 50-page inspection report. A vision model reads your appliance nameplate. And everything goes through a human review step before it's saved.

**[RESPONSIBLE AI — 10 seconds]**

That human-in-the-loop design is intentional. AI proposes, you approve. Every extraction is cached for review. Every API call is cost-tracked. No surprises.

**[BUSINESS VALUE — 10 seconds]**

For contractors, HomeDoc is a client management platform with bid comparison, referral networks, and embeddable widgets that generate leads. For partners, it's home intelligence that plugs into any website.

**[CLOSING — 5 seconds]**

HomeDoc. Your home, finally understood.
