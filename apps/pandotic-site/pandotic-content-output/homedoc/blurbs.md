## Medium Portfolio Blurb (108 words)

Pandotic designed and built HomeDoc, a comprehensive home management platform that replaces the filing cabinet and spreadsheet approach to homeownership with AI-powered intelligence. The system uses four AI models — each selected for specific strengths — to extract structured data from utility bills, inspection reports, warranties, and more. Environmental risk scoring aggregates five federal and state data sources into composite wildfire, flood, and climate assessments. A Total Cost of Ownership engine compares 17 HVAC systems with federal and state incentive lookup. For contractors and partners, HomeDoc offers a full B2B portal and 17+ embeddable widgets with lead capture — making home intelligence a distribution channel, not just a product.

## Sales-Oriented Blurb (95 words)

Built in a fast, iterative engagement, HomeDoc demonstrates what practical AI looks like in a complex domain. Rather than one monolithic AI model, the platform orchestrates four specialized services — each handling the document types it's best at — with human review gates and per-call cost tracking. The architecture is designed for growth: new APIs integrate via database migration alone, new modules plug into a standardized manifest system, and any feature can be embedded on a partner site in minutes. This is AI that ships, scales, and generates leads — not just a demo.
