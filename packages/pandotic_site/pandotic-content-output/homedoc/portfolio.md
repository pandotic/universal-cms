# HomeDoc — Portfolio Entry

## AI-Powered Home Intelligence Platform

HomeDoc is a comprehensive home management platform that uses multi-model AI to extract document data, score environmental risks from five federal sources, and help homeowners make informed decisions about maintenance, energy, and safety. Built for homeowners, contractors, and platform partners, it includes 14 feature modules, 17+ embeddable widgets, and a full contractor portal.

- **Reduced data entry burden** by auto-extracting structured information from 8 document types using the right AI model for each task — from utility bills to 50-page inspection reports
- **Delivered wildfire risk intelligence** no single data source can provide, compositing FEMA, NIFC, USGS, NOAA, and CAL FIRE data into actionable scores with an 11-stage defense certification workflow
- **Created a partner distribution channel** through 17+ embeddable widgets with email-gated lead capture, webhook delivery, and white-label branding — turning home intelligence into a lead generation engine
